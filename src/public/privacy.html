Privacy Policy

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information Collection And Use

We are using several different types of information that are provided by Slack API for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you. These include:

  • Workspace Channels - Livly need to join one channel to send notifications to your team
  • Workspace Users - Livly needs to know all users of team to provide them with opportunity to create leave
  • Leave information - Livly will track all your leave to provide you with rich features

From all information provided by Slack API, only the list of users on the workspace is saved in the database to provide user tagging in the notifications. This data is deleted when the app is deleted from the workspace.

We are not collecting any information about you and your profile, which purpose is not to provide our service to you and your teammates. All information that is collected is entered by users manually, by creating leave requests. Any leave request that is created can be viewed and deleted, so users have full access to all data entered.
